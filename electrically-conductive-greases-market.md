<p><p>Electrically conductive greases are specialized lubricants designed to reduce friction while maintaining electrical conductivity. Lithium-soap electrically conductive greases are formulated with lithium-based thickeners, offering excellent thermal stability, water resistance, and mechanical durability, making them ideal for high-load applications. Silica-based electrically conductive greases use silica as a thickening agent, providing superior oxidation resistance and stability at high temperatures, suitable for sensitive electronic components. Both types ensure reliable electrical connections and protection against corrosion, catering to diverse industrial and electronic applications.</p></p>
