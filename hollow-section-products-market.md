<p><ul><li>Hot-Finished Hollow Sections</li><li>Seamless Hollow Sections</li><li>Welded Hollow Sections</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hollow section products market comprises three main types: hot-finished hollow sections, seamless hollow sections, and welded hollow sections. Hot-finished hollow sections are shaped and formed at high temperatures, ensuring strength and dimensional accuracy. Seamless hollow sections are produced without joints, offering superior pressure resistance and integrity for critical applications. Welded hollow sections are made by joining steel plates through welding, providing versatility and cost-effectiveness for various construction and structural projects. Each type serves distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
