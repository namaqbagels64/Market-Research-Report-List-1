<p><p>The Acoustic Materials Market includes various types such as ABS, Fiberglass, PP, PU, and PVC, each serving specific soundproofing and noise control applications. ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene) offers durability and impact resistance, ideal for industrial settings. Fiberglass provides excellent sound absorption and thermal insulation, commonly used in construction. PP (Polypropylene) is lightweight and moisture-resistant, suitable for automotive and consumer goods. PU (Polyurethane) offers flexibility and superior sound dampening, used in interiors. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) is cost-effective and versatile, widely applied in building acoustics and flooring.</p></p>
