<p><p>The main bearing market consists of two primary types: sliding bearings and rolling bearings. Sliding bearings, also known as plain bearings, rely on a smooth surface for motion, providing low friction and supporting heavy loads. In contrast, rolling bearings utilize rolling elements, such as balls or rollers, to minimize friction and enhance efficiency, making them ideal for applications requiring high speed and precision. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs, including automotive, aerospace, and machinery sectors.</p></p>
