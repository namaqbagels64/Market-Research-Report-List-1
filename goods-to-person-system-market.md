<p><p>Goods-to-Person (G2P) systems enhance warehouse efficiency by bringing items directly to operators, minimizing travel time. Automatic Storage and Retrieval Systems (AS/RS) automate inventory handling, using robots or automated cranes to store and retrieve goods in high-density storage. Vertical Lift Modules (VLM) utilize vertical space, with trays that move up and down to deliver items, optimizing floor space. Horizontal Carousels consist of rotating shelves that bring products to the worker, ensuring rapid access and improved order fulfillment.</p></p>
