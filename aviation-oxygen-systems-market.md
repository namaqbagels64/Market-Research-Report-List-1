<p><p>The aviation oxygen systems market includes two primary types: Airborne Modular Oxygen Systems and Portable Liquid Oxygen Systems. In contrast, Portable Liquid Oxygen Systems offer a compact, light-weight solution for individual use, allowing for easy transport and refillability, catering primarily to the mobility needs of passengers requiring supplemental oxygen during air travel.</p></p>
