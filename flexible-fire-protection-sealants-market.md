<p><strong>The Flexible Fire Protection Sealants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Elastometric Type</li><li>Intumescent Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flexible fire protection sealants market is differentiated mainly into two types: elastomeric and intumescent sealants. Elastomeric sealants are designed to remain flexible, allowing for movement in joints and gaps while providing fire resistance. They maintain their integrity under varying conditions. In contrast, intumescent sealants expand when exposed to heat, forming a char that effectively seals off openings against fire and smoke. Both types are essential for maintaining safety and compliance in construction and renovation projects, catering to different fire protection needs.</p></p>
