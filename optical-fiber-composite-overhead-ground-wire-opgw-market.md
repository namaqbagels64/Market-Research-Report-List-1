<p><strong>The Optical Fiber Composite Overhead Ground Wire (OPGW) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Layer Stranding Structure OPGW</li><li>Central Tube Structure OPGW</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Optical Fiber Composite Overhead Ground Wire (OPGW) market is categorized primarily into two types based on their structural design: Layer Stranding Structure OPGW and Central Tube Structure OPGW. Layer Stranding Structure OPGW features optical fibers arranged in multiple layers around a central core, providing enhanced flexibility and strength. In contrast, Central Tube Structure OPGW houses fibers within a central tube, offering better protection against external forces. Both structures serve to deliver reliable data transmission while also functioning as overhead ground wires for electrical networks.</p></p>
