<p><p>The Global Navigation Satellite System (GNSS) market comprises various types of systems that enhance positioning accuracy globally. Global Constellations Systems, like GPS and GLONASS, provide worldwide coverage. Regional Constellations Systems, such as QZSS and EGNOS, offer services in specific areas to improve performance. Satellite-Based Augmentation Systems (SBAS) enhance GNSS accuracy by correcting signal errors, primarily for aviation and transportation. Together, these systems cater to diverse applications, including navigation, mapping, and timing across various sectors.</p></p>
