<p><p>The Automatic Dispensing Cabinets (ADCs) market is categorized into centralized and decentralized types. Centralized ADCs are typically located in a central pharmacy, allowing for efficient medication management and inventory control, often involving bulk storage and dispensing for multiple departments. In contrast, decentralized ADCs are situated in clinical areas, providing immediate access to medications at the point of care, enhancing workflow and patient safety by reducing wait times and improving medication availability. Both types aim to optimize medication distribution and administration efficiency.</p></p>
