<p><p>The infrared lamps market is categorized into three main types based on wavelength: Near Infrared, Medium Infrared, and Far Infrared. Near Infrared (NIR) lamps emit shorter wavelengths and are often used in applications like skin therapy and food processing. Medium Infrared lamps operate at intermediate wavelengths, suitable for industrial and heating applications. Far Infrared (FIR) lamps, emitting longer wavelengths, are commonly used for therapeutic heating and saunas, leveraging deep tissue penetration for various wellness applications.</p></p>
