<p><ul><li>Impulse Relays</li><li>Monitoring Relays</li><li>Signal Conditioning</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The control relays market can be categorized into impulse relays, monitoring relays, and signal conditioning relays. Impulse relays are designed for quick operations, providing short-duration outputs for applications like automation. Monitoring relays oversee system parameters, ensuring conditions stay within safe limits, and trigger alarms or actions when necessary. Signal conditioning relays enhance and modify input signals, improving signal quality for further processing. Together, these relay types play crucial roles in automation, safety, and signal management across various industries.</p></p>
