<p><p>The tungsten electrode market comprises several types, each suited for specific applications. Pure tungsten offers excellent corrosion resistance but limited performance in high-current applications. Thoriated tungsten, enhanced with thorium, provides better electron emission and arc stability. Lanthanum tungsten improves performance at lower temperatures, while cerium tungsten is known for its low ignition voltage and versatility. Yttrium tungsten is increasingly utilized for its enhanced performance characteristics. Other types may include various alloys and specialty formulations tailored for niche applications.</p></p>
