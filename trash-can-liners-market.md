<p><p>The trash can liners market is categorized primarily by material type. Polyethylene (PE) liners are versatile and widely used for general waste. Low-Density Polyethylene (LDPE) is more flexible, ideal for heavier loads but prone to punctures. High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE) offers strength and durability while being thinner, making it suitable for lighter applications. Polypropylene (PP) liners are chemically resistant and strong, often used for specialized waste disposal, such as in laboratories or medical settings. Each type caters to specific needs, balancing strength, flexibility, and cost.</p></p>
