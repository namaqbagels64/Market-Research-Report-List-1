<p><p>The Asphalt Waterproof Coating market includes two main types: Liquid Asphalt Waterproof Coating and Dry Asphalt Waterproof Coating. Liquid coatings are applied in a fluid state, offering seamless coverage and flexibility, making them ideal for complex surfaces. Dry coatings come in pre-formed sheets or rolls, providing quick installation and durability, often used for flat or large areas. Both types effectively prevent water infiltration, with liquid coatings excelling in adaptability and dry coatings in ease of application and structural strength.</p></p>
