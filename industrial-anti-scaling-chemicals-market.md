<p><p>The industrial anti-scaling chemicals market is primarily divided into polymer-based and phosphonate-based categories. Polymer-based anti-scaling agents are designed to disrupt the formation of scale deposits by modifying the surface characteristics of water and minerals, providing effective protection in various industrial applications. Phosphonate-based anti-scaling chemicals, on the other hand, contain phosphorus and are highly effective in chelating metal ions, preventing scale formation in water systems. Both types play crucial roles in maintaining efficiency and prolonging the lifespan of equipment in industrial settings.</p></p>
