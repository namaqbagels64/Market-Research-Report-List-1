<p><p>The anti-rust preservative market is categorized into two main types: oil-soluble and water-soluble preservatives. Oil-soluble anti-rust agents form a protective layer on metal surfaces, providing long-lasting protection in harsh environments. They are ideal for heavy-duty applications and machinery. In contrast, water-soluble anti-rust preservatives are easier to apply and environmentally friendly, making them suitable for lighter applications and temporary protection. Both types cater to specific needs in industrial and commercial sectors, ensuring the longevity of metal components.</p></p>
