<p><ul><li>Synchronous Motors</li><li>Induction Motors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AC motor market is primarily divided into synchronous and induction motors. Synchronous motors operate at a constant speed, synchronizing with the supply frequency, making them ideal for applications requiring precision and efficiency. Induction motors, more commonly used, rely on electromagnetic induction to operate, offering ease of maintenance and robustness. The choice between these types depends on application requirements, efficiency, cost considerations, and performance characteristics, influencing their respective market demand and growth across various industries.</p></p>
