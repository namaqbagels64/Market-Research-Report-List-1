<p><ul><li>Engine Drive Sprayer</li><li>Motor Drive Sprayer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld sprayer market can be categorized into engine drive sprayers and motor drive sprayers. Engine drive sprayers are powered by gasoline or diesel engines, offering high-pressure output and extended operational capacity, making them suitable for large-scale applications. In contrast, motor drive sprayers utilize electric motors, providing a quieter and emission-free alternative, ideal for smaller tasks and residential use. Both types serve diverse agricultural, gardening, and industrial needs, catering to varying user preferences and operational requirements.</p></p>
