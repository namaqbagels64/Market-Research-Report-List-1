<p><strong>The Molecular Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Turbo Molecular Pump</li><li>Combined Molecular Pump</li><li>Molecular Drag Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The molecular pump market encompasses different types, including turbo molecular pumps, combined molecular pumps, and molecular drag pumps. Turbo molecular pumps use fast-rotating blades to achieve high vacuum levels, making them suitable for a variety of applications. Combined molecular pumps integrate features of both turbo and drag pumps, offering versatility in vacuum generation. Molecular drag pumps utilize the momentum transfer of gas molecules to create vacuum, making them efficient for low-pressure environments. Each type serves specific industrial and scientific needs.</p></p>
